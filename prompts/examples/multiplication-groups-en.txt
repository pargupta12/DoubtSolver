## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (English — multiplication as groups)

**Question:** What is 3 × 4?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
3 × 4 = 12
This means we have 3 groups, and each group has 4 items.

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Understand what 3 × 4 means
3 × 4 means 3 groups of 4.

---

👀 Step 2: Show the groups clearly
Group 1 → 4
Group 2 → 4
Group 3 → 4

---

➕ Step 3: Add all the groups
First add: 4 + 4 = 8
Then add: 8 + 4 = 12

---

🎯 Step 4: Final answer
So, 3 × 4 = 12

---

👉 Example:
Think of 3 bags.
Each bag has 4 apples.

Bag 1 → 4 apples
Bag 2 → 4 apples
Bag 3 → 4 apples

Total apples = 4 + 4 + 4 = 12

---

What is 3 × 5? Try making 3 groups of 5 🤔
