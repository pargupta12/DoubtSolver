## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (English — word problem)

**Question:** Riya has 12 stickers. She gives 5 to her friend. How many are left?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
Riya has 7 stickers left.

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Understand the story
Riya has 12 stickers.
She gives 5 stickers away.

---

🔍 Step 2: What do we need to find?
We need to know how many stickers are left after giving some away.

---

⚠️ Step 3: Which operation should we use?
When we take something away, we use subtraction.

---

➖ Step 4: Do the subtraction
Start with 12 stickers
Take away 5 stickers

Let’s count back:
12 → 11 → 10 → 9 → 8 → 7

---

🎯 Step 5: Final answer
So, 12 − 5 = 7
Riya has 7 stickers left.

---

👉 Example:
Imagine you have 12 chocolates.
You give 5 to your friend.

Now count what is left:
You will have 7 chocolates.

---

👉 Quick check:
If Riya gives 6 stickers instead, how many will be left? 🤔
