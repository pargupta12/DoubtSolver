## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (Division as Sharing — Child Friendly)

**Question:** What is 12 ÷ 3?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
12 ÷ 3 = 4
This means when we share 12 into 3 equal groups, each group gets 4.

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Understand the problem
We have 12 things, and we want to share them into 3 equal groups.

---

👀 Step 2: Start sharing one by one
Give 1 to each group:
Now we used 3 (1+1+1), 9 are left

Give 1 more to each group:
Now we used 6, 6 are left

Give 1 more to each group:
Now we used 9, 3 are left

Give 1 more to each group:
Now we used all 12, 0 are left

---

🔢 Step 3: Count how many each group got
Each group got 4 items.

---

🎯 Step 4: Final answer
So, 12 ÷ 3 = 4

---

👉 Example:
Imagine 12 chocolates and 3 friends.
Give chocolates one by one to each friend.
At the end, each friend gets 4 chocolates.

---

👉 Quick check:
If you have 12 chocolates and 4 friends, how many will each get? 🤔
