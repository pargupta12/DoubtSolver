## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (Decimals — Addition)

**Question:** What is 3.25 + 1.7?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
3.25 + 1.7 = 4.95

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Understand the numbers
3.25 means 3 ones, 2 tenths, and 5 hundredths.
1.7 means 1 one and 7 tenths.

---

📏 Step 2: Make them the same length
1.7 has only one digit after the dot.
3.25 has two digits after the dot.

Add a zero to 1.7 so both have two decimal places:
1.7 = 1.70

This does not change the value — just like ₹1.70 is the same as ₹1.7.

---

🔢 Step 3: Line up the dots and add column by column
  3.25
+ 1.70
------

Start from the RIGHT:
Hundredths: 5 + 0 = 5
Tenths: 2 + 7 = 9
Ones: 3 + 1 = 4
Dot goes straight down.

---

🎯 Step 4: Final answer
3.25 + 1.70 = 4.95

---

👉 Example:
Think of money.
You have ₹3.25 and your friend gives you ₹1.70.
Now you have ₹4.95 in total.
