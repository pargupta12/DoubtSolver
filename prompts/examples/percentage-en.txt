## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (Percentage — Finding % of a Number)

**Question:** What is 20% of 150?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
20% of 150 = 30

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Understand what "20% of 150" means
20% means "20 out of every 100."
So we need to find 20 out of every 100 — but our number is 150.

---

📐 Step 2: Write it as a fraction
20% = 20/100

---

🔢 Step 3: Multiply
20/100 × 150

First simplify: 20/100 = 1/5

So: 1/5 × 150 = 150 ÷ 5 = 30

---

✅ Step 4: Check with the shortcut
10% of 150 = 15 (just move the decimal one place left)
20% = double of 10% = 15 × 2 = 30 ✓

---

🎯 Step 5: Final answer
20% of 150 = 30

---

👉 Example:
If a class has 150 students and 20% are absent today,
then 30 students are absent.
That means 120 students came to school.

---

If a shirt costs ₹200 and there is a 25% discount, how much do you save? 🤔
