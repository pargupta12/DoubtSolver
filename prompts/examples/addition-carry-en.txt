## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (Addition with Carry — Child Friendly)

**Question:** What is 28 + 15?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
28 + 15 = 43
This means when we add 28 and 15, we get 43.

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Look at the numbers
We are adding 28 and 15.

---

🔢 Step 2: Start from the ones place
We add the last digits:
8 + 5 = 13

---

⚠️ Step 3: Understand what 13 means
13 means:
👉 1 group of ten
👉 and 3 ones

---

🔄 Step 4: Write and carry
We write 3 in the ones place.
We carry 1 to the tens place because it is one full ten.

---

➕ Step 5: Add tens place
Now add:
2 + 1 = 3
Then add the carried 1:
3 + 1 = 4

---

🎯 Step 6: Final answer
So the answer is 43.

---

👉 Example:
Think of 8 chocolates + 5 chocolates = 13 chocolates.
You can group 10 chocolates into one box.
So you keep 3 chocolates and move 1 full box to the tens place.

---

👉 Quick check:
What is 9 + 4? What do you carry? 🤔
