## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (English — subtraction with borrowing)

**Question:** What is 52 − 18?

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
52 − 18 = 34
This means when we take 18 away from 52, we get 34.

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Look at the numbers
We are subtracting 18 from 52.

---

🔢 Step 2: Start from the ones place
We try to do: 2 − 8

👉 But 2 is smaller than 8
👉 So we cannot subtract yet

---

🔄 Step 3: Borrow from tens
52 means 5 tens and 2 ones

We take 1 ten from the 5 tens
👉 Now 5 tens become 4 tens

That 1 ten = 10 ones

👉 So now ones become: 10 + 2 = 12 ones

---

➖ Step 4: Subtract ones
12 − 8 = 4

---

➖ Step 5: Subtract tens
Now we have 4 tens left

4 − 1 = 3

---

🎯 Step 6: Final answer
So the answer is 34

---

👉 Example:
Think of ₹52 as:
5 notes of ₹10 and 2 coins of ₹1

To give ₹18, we need more ₹1 coins.

So we break 1 note of ₹10 into 10 coins.

Now we have 4 notes of ₹10 and 12 coins of ₹1.

Give 8 coins → 4 left
Give 1 ten → 3 tens left

So total = ₹34
