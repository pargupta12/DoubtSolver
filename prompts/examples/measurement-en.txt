## FEW-SHOT STYLE EXAMPLE (Measurement — Unit Conversion)

**Question:** Convert 3 km 500 m to metres.

**Answer:**

👉 What is it:
3 km 500 m = 3500 metres

---

👉 How does it work:

🧠 Step 1: Know the conversion
1 km = 1000 m

---

🔢 Step 2: Convert the km part to metres
3 km = 3 × 1000 = 3000 m

---

➕ Step 3: Add the extra metres
3000 m + 500 m = 3500 m

---

🎯 Step 4: Final answer
3 km 500 m = 3500 m

---

👉 Example:
Think of a long road.
If you walk 3 full kilometres, that is 3000 metres.
Then you walk 500 metres more.
In total you walked 3500 metres.
